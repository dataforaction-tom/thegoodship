Shipyard

Your organisation has problems that software could solve. Shipyard is how we discover them, design the right tools, build them properly, and ship them — fully deployed, documented, and yours to keep.

the idea

Tools you own, built around how you actually work

You know the feeling. The spreadsheet that started as a quick fix and now runs half your operations. The process that falls apart when one person is on holiday. The report that takes someone a full day to pull together every quarter. The platform you're paying for that nobody really uses.

These aren't technology problems. They're design problems. The tools you have weren't built around how your team actually works — so your team works around the tools instead.

Shipyard fixes this. We come in, figure out where the real problems are, design tools that fit how your team actually operates, and build them using modern, AI-assisted approaches. We favour open-source software and EU-based alternatives that strengthen your tech and data sovereignty — reducing dependency on big vendors and improving resilience, but we are pragmatic, building what you need, how you need it. No bloated platforms. No eye-watering licence fees. Tools you own, that do the job, and that your people can actually use.

two approaches

This is the right choice when building internal capability matters as much as the tools themselves.

A note on technology choices

We don't build on Microsoft. Not SharePoint, not Power Platform, not Dynamics. The development cycles are too long, the licensing costs are too high, the integration headaches are too frequent, and the results rarely justify the pain.

Instead, we use open-source software and EU-based alternatives wherever possible. This isn't just a technical preference — it's about sovereignty. Your data stays under your control, hosted where you choose, governed by regulations that protect you. You're not locked into a vendor's roadmap, pricing changes, or terms of service. Open tools are faster to build with, cheaper to run, more transparent, and genuinely resilient. Your organisation will be stronger for it.

The Slow Post

Switch off. Go outside. Write something by hand. Then post it to someone.

Wednesday 10 June 2026 · World Environment Day
the idea

How it started

I've been at a screen a lot. I am now. It's the nature of what I do.

But I've been thinking about the tangibility of things — slowing down, switching off. And I wondered if anyone else felt the same.

So on Wednesday 10 June, during Great Big Green Week, I'm going to switch off, go outside with a pen and a card, and write something. And I wondered if anyone wanted to do the same and and then we post each other the card. Via The Slow Post.

A few of people also thought this sounded like a good idea, so I thought I better follow through. So here we are.

It's a small experiment. A pause. A moment to step away from the screen and do something with your hands. To connect with someone else in a simple, tangible way.

You can take part in a few ways, in spirit, actually posting physical cards, or sharing photos of your creations.
